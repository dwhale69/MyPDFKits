FAQ

Common questions

Is MyPDFkits really free?
Yes — completely free. All 9 tools, unlimited use, no registration, no watermarks on output. We keep it free with zero-infrastructure overhead since everything runs in your browser.
Are my PDFs safe? Do you store them?
Your files never leave your device. All PDF processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. There is no server, no upload, and nothing stored — not even temporarily. Once you close the tab, the file is gone.
What's the maximum PDF file size?
There's no hard limit from our side — since processing is local, the limit is your device's memory. Most modern devices handle PDFs up to 500 MB comfortably. Very large files (1 GB+) may be slow on lower-end devices.
Do I need to install anything?
Nothing. MyPDFkits runs entirely in your browser using modern web APIs. No extensions, plugins, desktop apps, or accounts are needed. Works in Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and all modern browsers.
Can I use it on my phone or tablet?
Yes. MyPDFkits is fully responsive and optimised for mobile. Upload, process, and download PDFs from your iPhone, Android, or tablet — the interface adapts to your screen size automatically.
Does it work offline?
Once the page has loaded, the core tools work without an internet connection since all processing is in-browser. You do need internet to initially load the page and its scripts.
